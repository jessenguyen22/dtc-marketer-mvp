# Social Strategy Launch

Build a posting rhythm that compounds. Resist the firehose.

## When to invoke
- Launching a brand handle on IG / TikTok
- "Our organic is flat — restart it"
- Onboarding a social media manager
- Pre-launch (build audience before SKU drops)
- Repositioning the brand voice on social

## Brand Brain context required
- `{{brand.voice}}` — sets posting tone + caption style
- `{{brand.audience}}` — JTBD + identity drives content pillars
- `{{brand.product}}` — features that demo well on short-form
- `{{brand.channel_strategy}}` — role of organic vs paid

## Inputs from user
1. Current handle stats (followers, avg views, engagement, top posts)
2. Production capacity (videos / stills per week, in-house vs agency)
3. Platforms in scope (IG / TikTok / YouTube Shorts / Pinterest)
4. Goal (audience growth / community / sales / UGC pipeline)
5. Anti-goals (e.g. "we will not chase trending audio that's off-brand")

## Process
1. **Pick 4 content pillars** — each maps to a JTBD or identity. Examples: education, behind-the-brand, customer story, point-of-view (POV) take.
2. **Set cadence per platform** — realistic to capacity. Better 3 great posts than 7 mediocre.
3. **Define format mix per pillar** (short video / carousel / still / live).
4. **Build a 30-hook starter library** — 30 reusable opening lines, tagged by pillar.
5. **Set the engagement loop** — reply window (within 1 hour of post), DM strategy, reply-to-reply tactic for reach.
6. **Growth math** — what follower growth + view growth target is realistic with chosen cadence.
7. **30-day kickoff plan** — week-by-week milestones.

## Output template

```
SOCIAL STRATEGY — <brand>

CONTENT PILLARS
| # | Pillar | Maps to | Format mix | % of posts |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Education       | <JTBD>     | reel / carousel | 30% |
| 2 | Behind-the-brand| identity   | reel / story    | 25% |
| 3 | Customer story  | objection  | reel + carousel | 25% |
| 4 | POV / hot take  | community  | static + reel   | 20% |

POSTING CADENCE
| Platform | Posts/week | Stories/day | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| IG       | 5  | 3-5 | 3 reels + 1 carousel + 1 still |
| TikTok   | 7  | —   | 1/day, batch-shoot 2x/week |

HOOK LIBRARY (30 starter hooks)
Pillar 1 — Education:
  - "The thing nobody tells you about <category>…"
  - "Stop doing <X>. Do <Y> instead."
  - "<n> ways <category> products lie to you"
  …

Pillar 2 — Behind-the-brand:
  - "Day in the life of building <brand>…"
  - "Why we said no to <industry-norm>"
  …

Pillar 3 — Customer story:
  - "<customer name> bought <product> because…"
  …

Pillar 4 — POV / hot take:
  - "Hot take: <controversial position>"
  …

ENGAGEMENT LOOP
- Reply to every comment in first 60 minutes after post
- DM thank-you + soft question to highly-engaged commenters (<50 follower accounts)
- Reply-to-reply: reply to top 3 comments with a follow-up to extend conversation

CAPTION STRUCTURE
- Hook line (≤8 words)
- Context (≤2 sentences)
- Specific or proof
- CTA (comment / save / share)
- 3-5 niche hashtags (no #love)

GROWTH MATH (90-day target)
- Cadence: <posts/week>
- Avg view target: <…>
- Realistic follower growth (organic only): <…>
- Top-of-funnel UGC pipeline: <…> creators DMed/wk

Week 1: <…>
Week 2: <…>
Week 3: <…>
Week 4: <…>

ANTI-GOALS
- <…>
- <…>
```

## Output checklist
- [ ] 4 pillars (not 8) each tied to a JTBD or identity
- [ ] Cadence realistic to stated production capacity
- [ ] Hook library has ≥30 hooks, tagged by pillar
- [ ] Engagement loop with first-hour reply rule
- [ ] Caption structure spec'd
- [ ] Growth target stated as a range with assumption
- [ ] 30-day plan has weekly milestones, not vague
- [ ] Anti-goals listed (focus = saying no)

## Anti-patterns
- Posting daily when production can't sustain it (3 weeks in, quality crashes)
- 8 pillars (no pillar gets enough volume to be recognized)
- "We'll post when inspired" — kills compounding
- Ignoring DMs and replies (the engagement loop is the growth lever)
- Copying competitor formats wholesale (no point of difference = no recall)
